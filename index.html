Analysis Frameworks

Rigorous analytical tools to transform data into strategic insights

  • SWOT Analysis
    Classic framework with strategic implications that drive action — not just four quadrants of bullet points.
  • Porter's Five Forces
    Competitive analysis examining rivalry, new entrants, substitutes, buyer power, and supplier power in the cultural sector.
  • Business Model Canvas
    Visual mapping of value propositions, customer segments, channels, revenue streams, resources, activities, and partnerships.
  • Revenue Mix Analysis
    3-year trending of all revenue sources against 60/40 contributed/earned benchmark for museums.
  • Donor Pipeline Analysis
    Segmentation by giving level, retention rate analysis, and 80/20 rule application for major gift focus.

Quality Assurance Process

Gate 1: Discovery Complete

Gate 2: Analysis Complete

SWOT and competitive analysis done, financial benchmarking complete, strategic themes identified.

Gate 3: Draft Deliverables

All four deliverables in draft, financial models validated, internal review completed, board presentation done.

Gate 4: Final Deliverables

Client feedback incorporated, professional formatting complete, Mellon requirements verified, sign-off obtained.
